# The Daily Reset — Launch Pack

## 1. Prospect List Criteria

### Ideal customer profile

- **Who:** Senior executives, founders, C-suite, general managers, directors
- **Where:** LinkedIn, executive networks, industry associations
- **Signals:**
  - Title contains: CEO, COO, CFO, CTO, Founder, Owner, Managing Director, General Manager, VP, Partner, Principal
  - Company size: 10-500 employees (big enough to have pressure, small enough that the exec feels it personally)
  - Industry: Any — but professional services, tech, manufacturing, and healthcare executives tend to feel burnout most acutely
  - Activity signal: Posts about being busy, overwhelmed, hiring challenges, team management, long hours, travel
  - LinkedIn signal: Recently promoted (new pressure), or in role 2+ years (sustained pressure)

### Where to find them

1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** (if available) — search by title + company size
2. **LinkedIn regular search** — type "CEO" or "Founder" + your industry
3. **Executive associations** — YPO, EO, Vistage, CEO Institute groups
4. **Your own network** — current and past connections who are executives
5. **Industry event attendee lists** — conferences, summits, trade shows

### Your first 10 prospects

Pick from your immediate network first — people who already know you. Warm outreach converts 5-10x better than cold. Start with:

1-3. Former business associates who are still in executive roles
4-5. Current LinkedIn connections with CEO/Founder titles
6-7. People in your EO/YPO/Vistage network (if applicable)
8-10. Second-degree connections introduced by mutual contacts
